DigitPlot for macOS

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026

Overview

DigitPlot is a local macOS utility for digitizing data from figure images. The app does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

Data Collection

DigitPlot does not collect personal information, analytics, advertising identifiers, location data, contact information, or usage tracking data.

Local File Processing

DigitPlot processes user-selected images locally on your Mac. Exported CSV files are created only when you choose to export them. Imported images and exported files remain under your control.

Accounts

DigitPlot does not require an account, user login, or server-side profile.

Purchases

DigitPlot uses Apple's StoreKit for the 7-day trial state, one-time full unlock purchase, and purchase restoration. Apple may process purchase information according to Apple's own privacy policies and App Store terms. DigitPlot does not receive or store your payment card information.

Third-Party Tracking

DigitPlot does not include third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or DigitPlot support, email x.n.chen@outlook.com.
