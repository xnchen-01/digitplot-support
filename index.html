Common Questions

Do I need an account?

No. DigitPlot does not require a login account.

Where are imported images processed?

Imported images are processed locally on your Mac. DigitPlot uses files selected by the user for digitization and CSV export.

How does the trial work?

DigitPlot includes a 7-day full-feature trial. After the trial, the full version can be unlocked with a one-time in-app purchase through Apple's StoreKit purchase flow.

How do I restore a purchase?

Use Restore Purchases in DigitPlot. The app checks Apple's StoreKit entitlement for the full unlock.
